TRON LIGHTCYCLES: DIAGONAL EDITION

Welcome to the Grid, Program! This is classic Tron Lightcycles with a revolutionary twist - diagonal movement capability.

The Basics

Each player controls a lightcycle that leaves a deadly light trail behind it. Crash into any trail (yours or your opponent's) or the arena walls, and you're derezzed! Last cycle standing wins.

The Diagonal Twist

Unlike classic Tron, you can move in 8 directions instead of just 4! Simply hold down two movement keys simultaneously:

  • Player 1 (Cyan): Use W, A, S, D keys
  • Player 2 (Magenta): Use Arrow Keys

Diagonal Movement Examples

Northeast (W + D / ↑ + →)
Southeast (S + D / ↓ + →)

Strategic Advantages

  • Cut Corners: Move diagonally to make tighter turns and trap opponents
  • Escape Routes: More directions mean more options to avoid collisions
  • Complex Patterns: Create intricate trail patterns to confuse your opponent

Controls

  • SPACE: Start/Restart game
  • Speed Slider: Adjust game speed (Slow → Normal → Fast → Ludicrous)
  • Audio: Engine sounds rev with each direction change
